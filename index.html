Between Two Kings

Anne Boleyn is accused of adultery and imprisoned at the Tower. The very next day she is due to be executed at the hand of a French swordsman. It seems that nothing can change the tragic outcome. England will have a new queen before the month is out.

And yet… What if events conspired against Henry VIII and his plans to take a new wife? What if there were things that even Thomas Cromwell couldn’t control, things which would make it impossible for history to go to plan?

The year is 1536. History is about to be changed forever.

The old Anne Boleyn is dead. The new Anne is a different woman.
